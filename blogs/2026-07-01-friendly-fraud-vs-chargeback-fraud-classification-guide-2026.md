Terms like “friendly fraud” vs. “chargeback fraud” are often used interchangeably in ecommerce but they do not always mean the same thing. Actually, they often refer to two different types of chargeback dispute — and when merchants don’t know exactly which one they’re dealing with, it makes prevention much harder.
